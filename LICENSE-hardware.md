# Hardware License — CERN-OHL-S v2 (Strongly Reciprocal)

All hardware design files in this repository (the OpenSCAD/CAD sources and exported
STL/STEP/DXF in `hardware/`) are licensed under the CERN Open Hardware Licence
Version 2 — Strongly Reciprocal (CERN-OHL-S v2).

You may use, study, modify, manufacture, and distribute these designs, including
commercially. Because this is the Strongly Reciprocal variant, if you distribute a
modified design (or a product made from it), you must make your modified design
files available under the same CERN-OHL-S license. This keeps every improvement in
the open commons and prevents anyone from closing a derivative.

Full text: https://ohwr.org/cern_ohl_s_v2.txt
